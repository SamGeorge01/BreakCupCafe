The Story Behind Breakcup

Born on the highway. Built for the journey.

Every highway in Kerala hides a thousand untold stories. Truck drivers pushing through the night. Families on long journeys. Travellers chasing something they can't name. Workers leaving home before dawn.

We saw them. We saw the exhaustion in their eyes and the longing for something warm — not just in their hands, but in their hearts.

Breakcup was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a moment to break away from the rush. A cup of tea that says "you're not alone on this road." A smile from a stranger on wheels. A pause that makes the rest of the journey feel lighter.

We took an Ape Goods Auto and converted it into a moving cafe — carrying fresh tea, coffee, and snacks across the National Highways of Kerala. Starting at NH 44 in Palakkad, and rolling across 7+ cities in 6 months.

This isn't just a cafe. It's a feeling on wheels.
